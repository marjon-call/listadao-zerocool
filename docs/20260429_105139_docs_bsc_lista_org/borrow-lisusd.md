**提示**

最低借款金额为15 lisUSD。

我们建议保持借款在显示的阈值以下，以减少清算风险。

要借用 lisUSD：

1. 在借款页面，点击 **My Borrowed Funds** 下的 **Borrow**。


![](https://docs.bsc.lista.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1284749027-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FeuAQJMk753IWaCTi0zzi%252Fuploads%252F5UvBpuvPiSnbkRroWCCc%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3De75a797f-4131-4526-ad7a-7f14bcc0d90d&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=61d847e5&sv=1)

1. 在下一页，输入所需的 lisUSD 数量，然后点击 **Proceed**。


![](https://docs.bsc.lista.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1284749027-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FeuAQJMk753IWaCTi0zzi%252Fuploads%252Fkx9ZK6WiFCGMCgFhlOuN%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D88955032-3438-4a88-b029-41c12a43857e&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=595080b3&sv=1)

1. 在 MetaMask 中确认交易。


![](https://docs.bsc.lista.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1284749027-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FeuAQJMk753IWaCTi0zzi%252Fuploads%252FenxFsDJFxSJ7lLCSutTe%252FMetaMask2.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D87c05774-9ace-4ea4-ae0d-501ee748b7c1&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=abd79a5a&sv=1)

1. 交易完成后，点击 **Okay**，通过点击 **Add lisUSD to MetaMask** 将借来的 lisUSD 添加到 MetaMask，或者简单地返回到借款页面，查看你的借款 lisUSD。


![](https://docs.bsc.lista.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1970575934-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FqKEnrN8hBs4vtubIKxE5%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-29ff150ec520d74a8dcf84dfaa193770ba5d7bae%252Fimage%2520%2829%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=23ecd2c4&sv=2)
