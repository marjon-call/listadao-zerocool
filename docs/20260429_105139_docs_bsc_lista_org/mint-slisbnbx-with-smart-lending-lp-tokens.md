Starting now, providing BNB/slisBNB liquidity via Lista Smart Lending will also be eligible for minting slisBNBx.

Currently, some users may experience problems when depositing slisBNB as collateral:

![](https://docs.bsc.lista.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1802824789-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F60fXF9rALi6luWHVkkGg%252Fuploads%252FbsMfnX7fQT7IDtIkxsnP%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dcd1a8669-ca3b-4624-bc7d-98e4b0ec6f15&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=ca4f9b8e&sv=2)

To resolve this issue, click on “Manage slisBNBx” at the bottom or visit [this pagearrow-up-right](https://lista.org/lending/delegate?utm_source=gitbook&utm_medium=article&utm_campaign=mint-slisbnbx-with-smart-lending-lp-tokens):

![](https://docs.bsc.lista.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1802824789-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F60fXF9rALi6luWHVkkGg%252Fuploads%252FgrWZ1NAnc4YH2JKjjd2O%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D38ed54ca-0371-4044-97aa-edabece8d161&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=dd86d125&sv=2)

On our slisBNBx delegation page, delegate your slisBNBx to any other wallet that is controlled by you:

![](https://docs.bsc.lista.org/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1802824789-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F60fXF9rALi6luWHVkkGg%252Fuploads%252FSgXPkRs3RrObCxFlGvrG%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D47226fc4-8eba-476a-bf3c-b17ee9d09594&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=f8044cf3&sv=2)

Then, head back to the previous page where you wished to deposit slisBNB as collateral and make a deposit/withdrawal of any amount. Now, your slisBNBx will be delegated to your current wallet.
