### [hashtag](https://docs.bsc.lista.org/introduction/lista-lending/borrowers-and-suppliers\#supplier-lenders)    Supplier (Lenders)

Anyone can be a supplier - individuals, protocols, DAOs, or even hedge funds. They can either actively manage their positions by manually adjust their exposure in different markets, or take a more passive route by depositing into a vault aligned with their risk profile. The vault will then allocate funds across markets for the supplier.

### [hashtag](https://docs.bsc.lista.org/introduction/lista-lending/borrowers-and-suppliers\#borrowers)    Borrowers

Borrowers can select from a range of markets on Lista Lending based on their needs - such as preferred collateral types, loan asset, and favorable borrowing rates.
